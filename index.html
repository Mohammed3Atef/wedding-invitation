Our Journey

Our Journey Together

We never needed a coincidence to meet; fate had been quietly saving us for each other since the simplest moments of childhood. People used to say I was a difficult child. I was outgoing and full of life, yet I had boundaries no one was allowed to cross. I didn't like people getting too close; I couldn't stand rough teasing, and no one was ever allowed to touch my hair. Except her. She was the only exception.

"كانت منهَ الله...و الان هي منهُ الله عليّ"

While everyone else saw a stubborn little boy, she somehow saw something different. She would gently run her fingers through my hair, as if she had always known how to calm the restless side of me. With her tiny hands, I found a sense of comfort that no one else could ever give me. We grew up together, and so did the quiet bond between us. Looking back now, it feels as though life was patiently preparing us for this moment—that the little girl who softened my childhood would one day become the woman who holds my heart. We didn't just grow up together. We grew into each other. She is not simply the love of my life. She is the story that began long before I understood what love was, and the story I hope will last forever.
